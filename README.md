# Retail Business Analytics Dashboard - Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is a **guided dashboard** completed as part of my data analytics training.  
It analyzes retail business performance across **sales, profit, and customer segments** using Power BI.

The objective was to transform raw data into interactive visuals and derive actionable business insights.

## 📈 Key Insights

### Sales
- Total Sales: **$12.64M** (+51.54% YoY)  
- Top Category: **Technology ($4.7M)**  
- Consumer segment contributes **51%+ of total sales**  
- Average Order Value: **$504.99**

### Profit
- Total Profit: **$1.47M** (+52.34% YoY)  
- Most Profitable Product: **Canon imageCLASS ($25K)**  
- Average Profit per Order: **$58.62**

### Customers
- Total Customers: **1,590**  
- Total Orders: **25K**  
- Total Products: **3,797**  
- Most Profitable Region: **England ($100K)**  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Invest further in the Technology category to sustain and scale revenue growth  
- Identify and address underperforming regions to improve overall profitability balance  
- Optimize pricing and cost structure for low-margin products to increase profit margins  
- Strengthen targeting and retention strategies for the Consumer segment, which drives over 51% of total sales
